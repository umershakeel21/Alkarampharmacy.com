Community Drug store

By offering high-quality and reasonably priced products and taking a proactive and front-line role in promoting public health, we will establish ourselves as Pakistan's and more specifically Khyber Pakhtunkhwa's premier retail and community pharmacy, providing the best pharmacy care services to the general public. We will rank among the top training and service providers for experts in a variety of fields, including pharmacists, company owners, managers, and administrators. Our goal is to offer a full line of high-quality, authentically sourced pharmaceutical, herbal, nutraceutical, surgical, and cosmetic items at affordable prices.

Providing 20+ hours services

At our medical center, we understand the importance of providing round-the-clock service to our clients. Medical emergencies can occur at any time, and patients need immediate access to medical care and attention. Therefore, we have implemented a 24-hour service to cater to the needs of our clients. Our team of healthcare professionals is available at all hours to provide medical care and support to our patients. We offer a range of medical services, including emergency care, outpatient services, diagnostic testing, and consultations. Patients can reach us through our emergency hotline or visit our medical center at any time. Our healthcare professionals are trained to handle medical emergencies and provide critical care to patients in need. We also provide after-hours consultations with our doctors and specialists to address patients' concerns and queries. By providing 24-hour service, we aim to offer comprehensive medical care and support to our clients, ensuring their well-being and satisfaction.
