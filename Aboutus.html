Corporate Profile a bit more about who we are... One of the top pharmacies in Peshawar, Al-Karam Pharmacy offers pharmaceutical and nutraceutical items, cosmetics, and toiletries to the general public through retail trade. Additionally, the pharmacy offers its customers community pharmacy services such as over-the-counter medicines, patient education, counselling, and first aid services with the assistance of trained and knowledgeable personnel such as pharmacists, pharmacy technicians, and health technicians with experience in the retail industry. Fresh business administration graduates as well as recent pharmacy graduates are given internship and training opportunities at Al-Karam Pharmacy in the area of retail and community pharmacy.

Our goal is to offer a full line of pharmaceutical, herbal, nutraceutical, surgical, and cosmetic items from reliable sources at affordable prices.
