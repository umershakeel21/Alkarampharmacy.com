One of the top pharmacies in Peshawar, Alkaram Pharmacy offers pharmaceutical and nutraceutical items, cosmetics, and toiletries to the general public through retail trade. Additionally, the pharmacy offers its customers community pharmacy services such as over-the-counter medicines, patient education, counselling, and first aid services with the assistance of trained and knowledgeable personnel such as pharmacists, pharmacy technicians, and health technicians with experience in the retail industry. We also offer chances for internships and training to recent pharmacy graduates in the areas of retail and community pharmacy, as well as to recent business administration graduates. Our goal is to offer a full line of affordable pharmaceutical, herbal, nutraceutical, surgical, and cosmetic items.
